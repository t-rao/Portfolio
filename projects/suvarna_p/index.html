cancon- head & neck cancer conference

Head and Neck Oncology team at Cytecare cancer Hospitals invites You to the 2nd dedicated symposium on management guidelines for advanced Head and Neck Cancer in India

The symposium is designed to discuss scope for improvement in outcomes of Head & Neck cancers, from an Oncology as well as organ functional perspective. The conference will include debate controversal topics, interactive sessions with audience participation, interactive panels, guest lecture and surgical videos.

highlights of the event

Multidisciplinary Oncology leaders

7 Subsite Specific Sessions

2 Key Note Addresses

6 Debates

7 Interactive Panels

2 Surgical Video Sessions

Faculty Interaction

1 Invited Lecture

who should attend

Residents, advanced Trainees and practicing surgeons and Physicians inthe field of Otolaryngology, Maxillofacial and General Surgery, Surgical, Radiation and Medical Onclogy, and Pain & Palliative Medicine with interest in Head and Neck Oncology.
